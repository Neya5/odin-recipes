Recipe of Chicken Fried

Let's try this recipe of Chicken Fried for an adorable family dinner, you will like it !

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • All purpose flour
  • Paprika
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Place flour in a large plastic bag, Season flour with paprika (which helps to brown the chicken), salt and pepper.
  2. Dip chicken pieces in buttermilk then, a few at a time, transfer them into the bag with flour; seal the bag and shake to coat well.
  3. Place the coated chicken on a cookie sheet or tray, and cover with a clean dish towel or waxed paper
  4. LET SIT UNTIL THE FLOUR IS OF A PASTE-LIKE CONSISTENCY. THIS IS CRUCIAL
  5. Fill a large skillet (cast iron is best) about 1/3 to 1/2 full with vegetable oil. Heat until VERY hot.
  6. Put in as many chicken pieces as the skillet can hold. Brown the chicken in HOT oil on both sides
  7. When browned, reduce heat and cover skillet; let cook for 30 minutes (the chicken will be cooked through but not crispy)
  8. Remove cover, raise heat again, and continue to fry until crispy.
  9. Drain fried chicken on paper towels
