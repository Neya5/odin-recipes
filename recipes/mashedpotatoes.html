Recipe of Mashed Potatoes

Let's try this recipe of Mashed Potatoes for an adorable family dinner, you will like it !

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Garlic
  • Unsalted butter
  • Milk
  • Sour cream
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. First, peel and cut the potatoes. Chop them into large chunks
  2. Place the potatoes and garlic in a large pot and cover with cold water by 1 inch
  3. Add a tablespoon of salt, and bring the water to a boil over high heat
  4. Reduce the heat to medium and gently boil until the potatoes are fork-tender.
  5. When the potatoes are tender, drain them into a colander.
  6. Finally, mash!
  7. Then, pour in the milk and add the butter, salt, and pepper
  8. Season to taste, adding more butter, sour cream, and salt and pepper as neededSeason to taste, adding more butter, sour cream, and salt and pepper as needed
  9. Enjoy
