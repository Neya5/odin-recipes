Recipe of Lasagna

Let's try this recipe of Lasagna for an adorable family dinner, you will like it !

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Beef mince
  • Tomato sauce
  • Beschamel
  • Oignon

Steps

  1. First, cut the oignons and cook it until lightly brown in a pan
  2. Then, add the beef mince on a medium heat
  3. When beef is cooked, add the tomato sauce and set the heat to light
  4. Let slowly cook during 20 minutes
  5. You can now start doing the Beschamel
  6. Put a thin layer of bolognese in the oven dish and add a a layer of pasta
  7. Add bolognese on top of the pasta and a layer of beschamel on top of the bolognese
  8. Keep going until your oven dish is full
  9. You can even add gruyere on top of the dish
